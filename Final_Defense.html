Context

Introduction

Why the clinic needs a secure, accurate, and faster record-keeping workflow.

Introduction

4

Operational realities shaped the system: manual records, digital transformation, daily clinic workload, and web-based centralization.

  • Traditional Dental Record-Keeping: The clinic currently relies on physical files and index cards.
  • Digital Transformation: The workflow needs to shift from manual processing to an automated digital environment.
  • Operational Challenges: Large volumes of patient records create inefficiencies and retrieval delays.
  • Web-Based Record Management: Web technologies can centralize, protect, and organize clinical data.

Statement of the Problem

The existing process creates avoidable administrative friction and exposes clinic information to physical record risks.

Excessive Staff Workload

Manual filing and time-consuming data retrieval increase the burden on clinic staff.

Incomplete or Fading Information

Pencil-written entries may fade, while index cards can tear or become misplaced over time.

Storage and Security Limits

Physical cabinets consume space and do not provide digital backup or controlled access.

Objectives of the Study

General Objective

Provide a computerized platform that improves the accuracy, efficiency, and security of managing patient information for Flores Basa Dental Clinic.

Specific Objectives

  1. Analyze gathered data using fishbone diagrams to understand clinic challenges.
  2. Design system modules for general clinic operations.
  3. Develop the solution with PHP and MySQL for secure storage and retrieval.
  4. Test and evaluate using ISO/IEC 25010 standards.
  5. Prepare a comprehensive implementation plan.

Purpose and Description

Purpose
  • Lessen clinic staff workload.
  • Provide a secure computerized platform.
  • Serve as a digital bridge between patients and the clinic.
Capabilities
  • Secure access for authorized users.
  • Online appointment booking.
  • Patient records and dental history management.
  • Automated SMS and email notifications.
  • Dashboard for clinic performance tracking.

Functional Requirements

Patient Management

Record, update, and manage patient information, including personal details and dental history.

Appointment Scheduling

Automate booking procedures and send SMS/email reminders to patients.

Regulatory Compliance

Apply data protection through authentication, access control, and secure handling.

Functional Requirements (Cont.)

Reporting

Generate real-time reports such as patient treatment summaries and laboratory case progress.

System Administration

Manage user accounts, assign role-based access, and maintain backup procedures.

Non-Functional Requirements

Performance

Pages and data must load quickly with reasonable application response time.

Reliability

The system should operate consistently and save patient medical data without unexpected loss.

Security

Protect sensitive health data through encrypted credentials and access restrictions.

Non-Functional Requirements (Cont.)

Scalability

The system must remain stable with multiple users and continued database growth.

Usability

The interface must be intuitive and clearly labeled for non-technical clinic users.

Compatibility

The system must work across common browsers such as Chrome, Edge, and Safari.

Conclusions

Modernized Operations

The developed system supports daily clinic workflows and secures patient information.

Excellent Ratings

The system received excellent ratings from both technical evaluators and end users.

Recommendations

Modern Framework Migration

Refactor the native PHP codebase to Laravel or CodeIgniter for stronger scalability.

Online Payment Gateway

Integrate GCash or PayMaya for consultation deposits and reduced no-shows.

Dedicated Mobile App

Expand into Flutter or React Native for improved patient access on mobile devices.

Encrypted Cloud Backups

Establish automated backups to strengthen recovery and prevent data loss.
